# Strategic Direction: ClickPoint Consulting — "AI Applied. Australia Ready."

This campaign sits at a critical market moment. Australian SMEs are AI-curious but overwhelmed—they're seeing the hype, feeling the pressure, but don't know where to start or who to trust. Our job is to position ClickPoint as the practical, no-nonsense guide who speaks their language and understands their business realities. We're not selling "AI magic"—we're selling clarity, confidence, and a clear first step.

The organic social engine needs to do the heavy lifting on trust-building and thought leadership. LinkedIn is our primary conversion driver—that's where decision-makers live. Instagram/Facebook supports brand awareness and humanises the consultancy, but we're not chasing vanity metrics there. The paid strategy is lean and intentional: LinkedIn Lead Gen for high-intent prospects, Meta for top-of-funnel awareness with retargeting potential. Everything funnels toward one action: book the discovery call.

Three-month runway means we need momentum fast. Month one establishes credibility and captures early intent. Month two deepens engagement with proof points and case-style content. Month three pushes conversion hard with urgency messaging. Let's move.

## Key Messaging Pillars (Non-Negotiable Consistency)

Everyone on this campaign must stay aligned to these four pillars:

1. **Practical over hype.** ClickPoint helps businesses *apply* AI—not just talk about it. Every message should feel grounded and actionable.

2. **Australian context matters.** This isn't Silicon Valley. Reference local industries, compliance considerations, and business realities. "Built for how Australian businesses actually operate."

3. **Clarity is the product.** The audience is overwhelmed. ClickPoint's value is making AI understandable and showing where to start. "You don't need to know AI—you need to know what it can do for your business."

4. **Low-risk first step.** The discovery call is free, no-obligation, and designed to give value even if they don't proceed. Remove friction. "One conversation. Zero jargon. Real answers."
