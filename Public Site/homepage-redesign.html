Common questions

Everything you need
to know before we talk.

What is AI marketing automation?
AI marketing automation uses artificial intelligence to handle repetitive marketing and operations tasks automatically — from scoring and routing inbound leads, to generating campaign performance reports, to monitoring tender portals for bid opportunities. Instead of your team doing this work manually, an AI system does it continuously in the background and delivers finished outputs each day.
How is ClickPoint Consulting different from a traditional marketing agency?
Traditional agencies execute campaigns on your behalf. ClickPoint Consulting builds the intelligent infrastructure that makes your marketing and operations team 3–5× more productive. We design and deploy AI automation systems — lead scoring engines, campaign attribution models, content pipelines, tender monitoring tools — that run continuously and deliver finished work to your team without manual intervention.
How long does it take to set up a marketing automation system?
Most clients see their first automation system live within 4–6 weeks. We start with an audit to identify the highest-impact quick wins, then build and deploy in focused sprints. Full stack implementations covering multiple services typically take 8–12 weeks.
What size businesses does ClickPoint Consulting work with?
We work best with growth-stage and mid-market Australian businesses with 10–500 employees that have real marketing and operations workloads but lack the internal resources to build and manage automation systems themselves. Our clients typically include B2B services, professional services, SaaS, and construction or infrastructure firms who bid for government contracts.
Do I need technical knowledge to use the systems you build?
No. We build and manage the entire system for you. Your team receives finished outputs — a scored lead brief each morning, a campaign performance report each week, an AI-drafted content calendar — without needing to understand how the automation works. We handle all the technical setup, integration, and ongoing maintenance.
