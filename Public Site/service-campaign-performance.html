Common questions

Everything you need to know
about Campaign Performance.

How does AI campaign attribution work across multiple channels?
AI campaign attribution connects data from every touchpoint in a customer journey — paid search, social ads, organic content, email, and direct — and uses machine learning to assign credit to the channels that actually influenced the conversion. Unlike last-click attribution, multi-touch attribution gives your team an accurate picture of what's driving revenue so you can allocate budget correctly.
What platforms does the campaign performance system connect to?
We integrate with Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, TikTok Ads, GA4, HubSpot, Salesforce, and most major marketing and CRM platforms. All data is unified into a single performance view, updated automatically every 24 hours.
How often are campaign performance reports generated?
Campaign performance reports are generated automatically on a weekly cadence and delivered directly to your team via email, Slack, or your preferred channel. Reports include cross-channel attribution, budget efficiency metrics, and AI-generated recommendations — written and ready to act on, not raw data dumps.
Can the system automatically optimise campaigns?
The system surfaces prioritised optimisation recommendations automatically — budget reallocation opportunities, underperforming ad sets, audience fatigue signals, and bid strategy adjustments. Your team reviews and approves changes before they're implemented, giving you AI-powered insight with human oversight.
How long does it take to see results from campaign performance automation?
Most clients receive their first automated campaign performance report within 2 weeks of onboarding. Measurable improvements in ROAS typically appear within the first 4–8 weeks as the attribution model stabilises and optimisation recommendations are actioned.
