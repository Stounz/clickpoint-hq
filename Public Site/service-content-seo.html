Common questions

Everything you need to know
about Content & SEO.

Will AI-generated content rank in Google?
Yes — when it's done correctly. Google's systems evaluate content on quality, relevance, and E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness), not whether a human or AI wrote it. Our process uses AI to handle research, structure, and first drafts, then layers in subject-matter expertise, original insights, and brand voice. Our clients average a 94 Lighthouse content score and 38% year-on-year organic traffic growth.
How do you ensure AI content matches our brand voice?
We build a custom brand voice model trained on your existing content — tone, vocabulary, sentence structure, and messaging frameworks. Every piece of AI-assisted content is generated through this model and reviewed for brand consistency before publication. Over time, the model improves as it learns from approved content.
How many pieces of content do you produce each month?
Our standard content pipeline produces 12+ optimised pieces per month — a mix of long-form SEO articles, short-form social content, email sequences, and supporting assets. All pieces are keyword-researched, structured for search intent, and aligned to your content calendar.
How long before we see SEO results?
SEO results typically begin to show within 3–6 months as new content is indexed and starts to rank. Competitive keywords take longer; informational and long-tail keywords often rank within 6–12 weeks. Our content strategy prioritises quick-win opportunities alongside long-term authority-building content so you see early traction while the broader strategy matures.
What's the difference between content marketing and SEO content automation?
Traditional content marketing involves a team manually researching, briefing, writing, editing, and publishing content — a slow, resource-intensive process. SEO content automation uses AI to handle the repeatable parts of this workflow at scale, so your team focuses only on strategy and approval. The result is 3–5× more content output at a fraction of the cost, with consistent quality and SEO optimisation built into every piece.
