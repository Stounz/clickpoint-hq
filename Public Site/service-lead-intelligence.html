Common questions

Everything you need to know
about Lead Intelligence.

How does AI lead scoring work?
AI lead scoring analyses each prospect against your ideal customer profile — company size, industry, technology stack, hiring activity, intent signals, and behavioural data. Each lead receives a score from 0 to 100 and is ranked by outreach priority. Our system does this continuously across 20+ data sources, so your team always knows exactly who to contact first.
What data sources does the lead intelligence system monitor?
Our lead intelligence systems monitor LinkedIn for hiring and growth signals, job boards for expansion indicators, funding databases for investment activity, intent data platforms, news feeds for trigger events, and industry-specific directories. The exact mix is tailored to your ICP and industry.
How quickly can the lead intelligence system be set up?
Most lead intelligence systems are live within 3–4 weeks. We start with a one-hour ICP workshop to define your scoring criteria, then build and test the data pipelines, and deliver your first live brief within the month.
Does the system integrate with our CRM?
Yes. We integrate directly with HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, and most major CRMs. Qualified leads are automatically pushed into your CRM with enrichment data pre-populated — company details, ICP score, signal notes, and contact information — ready for your sales sequences to trigger immediately.
How is this different from buying a leads list?
Purchased lead lists are static and unqualified — the same list your competitors are buying. Our system monitors live signals to find companies actively showing buying intent right now. Every lead is scored against your specific ICP, enriched with current data, and delivered with context on why they're a priority today.
