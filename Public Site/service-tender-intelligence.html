Common questions

Everything you need to know
about Tender Intelligence.

What government tender portals does the system monitor?
Our tender intelligence system monitors AusTender (Commonwealth), NSW eTendering, VIC Tenders, QLD Government QTenders, SA Tenders, WA Tenders, and a range of local government and industry-specific procurement portals — 20+ sources in total. The exact portal mix is configured to match your target markets and service categories.
How does AI score tender fit for our business?
We build a custom capability profile for your business that defines your relevant service categories, industry experience, contract value range, geographic footprint, and past performance. The AI scores each new tender against this profile on a 0–100 scale, flags the most relevant opportunities, and explains why each tender is or isn't a strong fit — saving your BD team hours of manual triage each week.
Can the system help us write tender submissions?
Yes. For shortlisted tenders, the system can generate a first-draft submission framework using your capability statements, past performance examples, and the specific requirements of the tender document. Your BD team reviews and finalises the submission — the AI handles the structure, compliance checklist, and initial drafting so you can focus on the strategic differentiation that wins bids.
How much time does the tender intelligence system save?
Clients typically save 8+ hours per week in manual tender monitoring and triage. Instead of your BD team scanning multiple portals daily and manually assessing each opportunity, the system does this automatically and delivers only the relevant, scored opportunities — so your team spends time on bid strategy, not portal-checking.
Is the system suitable for both state and federal government tenders?
Yes. Our tender intelligence system covers both federal (AusTender) and all state/territory procurement portals, as well as local government and selected private sector procurement platforms. You can configure alerts by jurisdiction, agency, category, or contract value to focus on exactly the opportunities that matter to your business.
