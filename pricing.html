Common questions
Do I need to pay per workspace or per seat? +
You pay per client workspace. There's no per-seat pricing within your agency — your whole team shares access to ClickPoint HQ under one subscription.
Can I white-label the client workspace with my own branding? +
Yes — on the Enterprise agency plan, you can fully white-label ClickPoint HQ and client workspaces with your own logo, domain, and colour scheme. Clients see your brand, not ClickPoint's.
Do my clients know they're using ClickPoint? +
On Starter and Pro plans, the platform is co-branded. On Enterprise, it's fully white-labelled — your clients see only your agency brand. The "CMO" persona can be renamed to match your team too.
Can I charge clients more than the listed workspace price? +
Absolutely. The client workspace prices listed are what you pay ClickPoint. You set your own retail price to clients — keeping the margin as part of your retainer or as an add-on. We never get involved in your client billing.
What happens when I upgrade or downgrade a client's plan? +
Feature access adjusts immediately. When upgrading, new features unlock right away. When downgrading, the client retains access until their next billing cycle. No disruption to their data.
Is there a free trial? +
Yes — all agency plans include a 14-day free trial with full feature access. No credit card required. Client workspace subscriptions start when you send the first invite link.
